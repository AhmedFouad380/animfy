Blender for Beginners: From Zero to 3D Artist

Welcome to my beginner-friendly Blender course — the perfect starting point for anyone who wants to dive into the world of 3D design! Whether you're an aspiring artist, content creator, game developer, or just curious about 3D, this course will guide you step-by-step through the core tools and concepts of Blender.

You’ll learn everything from navigating the Blender interface to creating your first 3D models, adding textures, lighting scenes, animating objects, and rendering your final projects. I break down complex ideas into simple, practical lessons that are easy to follow — even if you’ve never touched 3D software before.

By the end of the course, you won’t just know how to use Blender — you’ll create real projects and build a solid foundation that prepares you for more advanced 3D work, whether it’s visual effects, animation, or game design.

No prior experience is needed. Just bring your curiosity, creativity, and willingness to learn — and I’ll help you turn that into real 3D skills. You'll also gain tips, shortcuts, and insights from my personal workflow that will save you hours and boost your confidence as a 3D artist.

Let’s start your journey into the 3D world together — see you inside the course!

What You'll Learn

Use Blender and understand it's interface.
Understand the principles of modelling.
Create 3D models with simple colors.
Learn the basics of animation.
Create your own materials.
UV map and unwrap your models.
Create animations for your objects.
